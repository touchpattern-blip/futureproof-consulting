The three findings

1 · 1/2" nuts: 370 vs 0

Model disagreement, not a count error. Old sheet: bolt-into-strut-nut, ~no loose nuts. Field: 3–4 loose nuts at every point.

RESOLVED → keep all 370 (trap 1/2" hw at any point regardless).

2 · Rod AND bolt, every point

Old sheet carried them as substitutes (bolt 110, rod 0). Both are wanted at each point for field flex on drop height.

RESOLVED → both at 110.

3 · Real order gaps

Wall bucket (20 wall brackets + 20 angle brackets + their strut nuts) was entirely missing. MFC-1213 was 15 short.

RESOLVED → wall bucket added, MFC corrected to 40.
