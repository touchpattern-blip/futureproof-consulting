Why this matters now

Centralized means revocable

Cloud AI is borrowed brilliance. It is fast, capable, and it disappears the moment a vendor, a regulator, or an outage decides it should. When a model vanishes for everyone at once, it is because it lived in one place with one hand on the valve.

Sovereignty is not paranoia. It is resilience. The goal is not to abandon frontier models. It is to make sure nothing you cannot afford to lose is structurally dependent on a faucet someone else controls.

I have spent 25 years making technically complex systems actually work, from immersive installations to show control. The same discipline applies here: understand the whole stack, find where the brief meets the physical world, and put the foundation on ground you hold.
