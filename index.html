Process

How I work

Four steps. No surprises.

You describe the project

Tell me what you're building. I'll ask a few questions, then I'll be honest: am I the right person, or would someone else serve you better? My reputation is built on honest fit assessment, not closing every engagement.

We scope the engagement

You get a specific proposal that shows I understood your project. Level of involvement, timeline, deliverables, and expectations. You know exactly what you're buying before anything is signed.

You have a senior technical partner

During the engagement, I own the technical layer. Regular check-ins, vendor coordination, and problem-solving. You focus on the creative and business side.

You keep the knowledge

System specs, vendor contacts, runbooks, documentation. The institutional knowledge is yours. It lives in your files, not in my head.
