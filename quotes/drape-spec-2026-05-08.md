## Notes

- Fabric fullness is sewn into carrier webbing at each station (shirring style). Fullness folds front-to-back between carriers when stacked.
- Track is ceiling-mounted directly to soffit via ADC 1423 clamps. Fastener specification dependent on soffit material (steel deck, concrete, or wood).
- ADC Rig-i-Flex 1400 cord-operated limits: 40' one-way / 60' bi-part. A 77' run may require motorization or multiple independent draw sections.
- All fabric is inherently flame retardant (IFR) -- permanent, will not wash out or degrade.
